Ingredients of Hibachi rice and chicken

  3. You need 1 teaspoon of onion powder.
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon of garlic powder.
  5. It's of salt and pepper.
  6. You need of soy sauce.
  7. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  8. Prepare of butter.

Hibachi rice and chicken step by step

  1. Cook 2 cups of rice in 2 1/4 plus cups of water, or to directions on rice.
  2. Cook chicken in medium skillet, don't overcook. 2.5 min each side on high, 12 min on 4.5. Flipping occasionally.
  3. Beat eggs in bowl, Coat large skillet in Pam or oil and cook eggs, chop them up and return to bowl.
  4. Coat large skillet with some butter and add vegetables.
  5. Add about half of the onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Add some soy sauce to color.
  6. Once veggies near cooked and chicken near cooked, cut up chicken into pieces and add to large skillet.
  7. Add rest of spices and some more soy sauce, cook for a few minutes, make sure chicken is done.
  8. Add egg back in.
  9. Add rice to skillet, add soy sauce so rice is evenly brown.
