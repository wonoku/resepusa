Quick Cooking ★ 30-minute Garlic Potato Gratin.

You can cook Quick Cooking ★ 30-minute Garlic Potato Gratin using 9 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Quick Cooking ★ 30-minute Garlic Potato Gratin

  1. Prepare 2 of handfuls Frozen potatoes (pre-cut for french fries).
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of Margarine.
  3. You need 1 of Onion.
  4. It's 2 slice of Bacon.
  5. You need 1 tsp of Garlic chips.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of White flour.
  7. You need 300 ml of Milk (as high in fat as possible).
  8. It's 1 1/2 tsp of Chicken bouillon or consommé.
  9. Prepare 2 of handfuls Shredded cheese.

Quick Cooking ★ 30-minute Garlic Potato Gratin instructions

  1. Place the potatoes in gratin bowls and microwave until warm. Alternatively, fry them in a frying pan, with a small amount of oil!.
  2. Meanwhile, fry the onion and bacon in margarine..
  3. Stir in about 2 tablespoons of flour, then add the garlic chips..
  4. Stir in the milk, a little at a time. Season with the chicken bouillon and black pepper. Let it simmer until it's quite thick..
  5. Pour the sauce over the potatoes..
  6. Top each dish with a handful of cheese..
  7. Bake in a oven-toaster or boiler (or fish grill) until it browns to a delicious-looking color..
