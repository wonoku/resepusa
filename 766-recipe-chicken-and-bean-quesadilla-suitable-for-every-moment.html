You can cook Chicken and Bean Quesadilla using 10 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Chicken and Bean Quesadilla

  1. Prepare of Cooking spray.
  2. It's 1 can of black beans.
  3. You need 2 lbs of chicken breast diced.
  4. You need 1 cup of diced red onion.
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of cumin.
  6. Prepare 8 oz of mushrooms.
  7. It's 16 of small corn tortillas.
  8. Prepare 2 cups of shredded cheddar.
  9. You need Packet of fiesta ranch dry mix.
  10. It's 2 cups of greek yogurt.

Chicken and Bean Quesadilla step by step

  1. Spray a pan with cooking spray and saut√© beans(drained and rinsed), onion, mushroom, cumin, and chicken for 5-6 min or until chicken is cooked through..
  2. Remove from pan and set aside..
  3. Spray pan again, place 1 tortilla in pan, put 2 tbsp of cheese on tortilla, then 1/2 cup of chicken mixture. Place another tortilla on top, cook for 2 min on both sides..
  4. Repeat for 7 more quesadillas..
  5. Cut into quarters..
  6. Mix the ranch packet and yogurt and spoon over quesadillas. Serve hot. (Taco sauce, salsa, or chipolte sauce are all good substitutes for the spicy ranch).
