Ingredients of Easy Dry Rub Chicken

  1. You need 4 of skin-on boneless chicken thighs.
  2. Prepare 2 Tablespoon of olive oil.
  3. Prepare 1 Tablespoon of Italian seasoning.
  4. You need 2 teaspoons of paprika.
  5. It's 2 teaspoons of garlic powder.
  6. Prepare 2 teaspoons of onion powder.
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.

Easy Dry Rub Chicken instructions

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients and set aside..
  2. Make a few slices on the flesh part of the chicken thigh without cutting through. Use a paper towel to pat dry the thighs..
  3. Rub both sides of the chicken with olive oil. Then, rub both sides of the chicken generously with the dry mix..
  4. Line the fryer basket with a lightly greased aluminum foil. Put the chicken thighs into the basket without stacking. Air fry at 360F (180C) for about 8 minutes. Then, air fry again at 400F (200C) for about 4 minutes until cooked through at 165F (74C)..
