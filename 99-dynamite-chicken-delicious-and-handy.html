Dynamite chicken instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into bite size cubes.
  2. Then put in a bowl and add all your spices and ingredients.
  3. Mix them up and marinate it for an hour.
  4. After that beat the eggs and and and separately mix the cornstarch,flour,salt,and pepper together.
  5. After that take your marinated chicken and dip it in the dry mixture then dip it into the eggs and put it in a pan with hot oil repeat the step with all of your chicken bites.
  6. Mix the special sauce together and dip your cooked chicken all over it and put it in a bowl of lettuce.
  7. Serve.
