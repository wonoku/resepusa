Herbal Chicken Breast.

You can cook Herbal Chicken Breast using 7 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Herbal Chicken Breast

  1. It's 2 pieces of Chicken Breast.
  2. It's 2 tbsp of Mayonnaise.
  3. You need 2 tsp of Garlic paste.
  4. You need 3 tsp of Parmesan Cheese.
  5. You need 4 tbsp of English Parsely (Fresh or Dried one).
  6. You need 200 ml of Crumbs Bread.
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp of Salad Oil (Cooking Oil).

Herbal Chicken Breast step by step

  1. Cut the chicken diagonally into thin, bite sized slices..
  2. Rub the Mayonnaise and Garlic paste into the chicken. Keep it for 30 minutes..
  3. Chop the English parsely. Combine the Parmesan Cheese, English Parsely and Crumbs Bread in a bowl..
  4. Thoroughly coat the chickens with the bread crumbs mixture. Rest the chickens for 5 minuets..
  5. If you want to frozen these, you put the chicken Breast in Zip bag with crumb breads..
  6. Heat oil in a frying pan, put the chicken breast pieces in the pan. (Not deep fry).
  7. Then both sides become golden brown, put on these a dish..
