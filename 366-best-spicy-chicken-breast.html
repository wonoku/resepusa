Ingredients of Spicy Chicken Breast

  1. It's 1 of chicken breasts.
  2. It's 1 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  3. It's 3 of chilies, chopped.
  4. It's 3 clove of garlic, chopped.
  5. It's 1 of olive oil.
  6. You need of salt.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of ground cloves.
  8. It's 1 of lemon juice.
  9. Prepare tsp of thyme.
  10. You need cup of parsley.
  11. You need 1 tsp of Worcestershire sauce.

Spicy Chicken Breast instructions

  1. Flatten and half the chicken breast in two parts.
  2. Score the chicken.
  3. In a bowl, mix chopped chilies, garlic, ground cloves and cinnamon, pepper, salt, parsley and thyme. Add lemon juice and drizzle with olive oil. Mix the spice mix..
  4. Rub the spices over the chicken breasts on both sides and keep in the fridge for 3-4 hrs.
  5. In a pan, heat olive oil and brown the chicken breasts for about 5-10 mins.
  6. Add Worcestershire sauce and stir..
  7. Cover the pan with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at 220Â°C for 20 mins. (Shreds of butter can be added on top of the chicken breasts before putting in the oven, to avoid drying of the chicken breasts.).
  8. Plate, garnish with parsley and serve..
