Ingredients of Chicken loves coconut milk and rice

  1. Prepare 1/2 cup of rice.
  2. You need 5 piece of mushroms.
  3. You need 1 large of chicken breast.
  4. You need 2/3 cup of coconut milk.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of lemon juice.
  6. You need 1 pinch of salt.

Chicken loves coconut milk and rice instructions

  1. Boil the rice for 10 minutes then drain the water..
  2. Cut the mushroms and put then in the hot pan with some salt.
  3. Cut the chicken breast small pieces and mix it in a bowl with a little salt, the lemon juice and 5 tablespoons of coconut milk and place it in the pan with the mushroms for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add to the pan the coconut milk and the rice and mix well and let it boil on medium heat for 10 minutes then high heat so that the excess liquit to evaporate and done..
