Fluffy's crispy gratin roast step by step

  1. Wash and dry chicken well. Prepare flour, egg and breadcrumb mixture.
  2. Coat each piece of chicken in flour, dust off the excess, then dip the chicken skin side down in egg, then roll in the breadcrumbs, sprinkle with salt.
  3. Preheat oven to 200. Put potatoes in a large baking tray lined with baking paper. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Start to bake while you fry off the chicken.
  4. Heat oil in a large pan. Make sure it's hot. Put chicken skin side down in the pan and crisp up the skin. It should take about 5-6 mins. Then remove potatoes and make room for the chicken.
  5. Add chicken to tray, sprinkle any left over breadcrumbs onto the potatoes. Give a shake and continue cooking on the oven for about another 40 mins until cooked and yummy üòç.
