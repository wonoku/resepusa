Grilled chicken breasts with barbeque sauce step by step

  1. Remove skin from chicken breast halves and place them in ziplock bag.
  2. Combine olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic, lemon juice in a small bowl and pour over chicken.
  3. Let chicken marinate for atleast an hour or overnight in fridge.
  4. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Remove chicken from bag and place on a baking sheet. Season with salt and pepper..
  5. Bake for 20 mins and brush a layer of barbeque sauce on the chicken. Return to the oven and repeat the brushing sauce every 5 minutes until chicken is cooked thoroughly..
  6. It will take 15 to 20 mins to get even cooking..
  7. Serve with some extra sauce on top with mayonnaise and barbeque sauce sauted vegetables.
