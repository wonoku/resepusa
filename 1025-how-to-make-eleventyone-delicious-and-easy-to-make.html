Eleventyone step by step

  1. Form and season the sirloin. Pan fry in a skillet.
  2. Add cheese on top.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of water and cover let the cheese melt.
  4. Add the onions to pan.
  5. Add wine and celery salt let simmer stirring occasionally.
  6. Cook till all the juices are gone set aside.
  7. Split the chicken in half add to the pan with 1/4 cup of oil.
  8. Turn after 3 and a half minutes. Add the rest of spices to the cooked side.
  9. Cook 4 minutes cover and add 2 tablespoons of water let steam 5 minutes.
  10. Toast the buns in a skillet.
  11. Start making the sandwiches.
  12. Serve I hope you enjoy!.
