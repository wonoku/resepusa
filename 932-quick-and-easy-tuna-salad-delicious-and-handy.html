Ingredients of Quick and Easy Tuna Salad

  1. You need 4 of x 5oz cans tuna (in water), well drained.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of mayonnaise.
  3. You need 1/3 cup of finely chopped celery (about 1 rib).
  4. You need 2 tablespoons of finely chopped red onion (about 2 slices).
  5. It's 2 tablespoons of sweet pickle relish or dill relish.
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon of fresh lemon juice.
  7. You need 1 of garlic clove, minced.
  8. You need of Salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  9. Prepare Pinch of dill.

Quick and Easy Tuna Salad step by step

  1. Add the first 7 ingredients to a bowl and mix well..
  2. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with a pinch of dill.
  3. Can be eaten straight away or for even better results, cover with and chill in the fridge for a couple of hours. Enjoy!.
