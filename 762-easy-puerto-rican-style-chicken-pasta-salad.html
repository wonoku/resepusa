Easy Puerto Rican style chicken pasta salad instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to box instructions. Drain. Do not run cold water on it. Pour approximately 4 tbsp and tossed until evenly coated so it won't stick..
  2. Chicken should be grilled and cubed. I season with a little lemon juice, Abode, pepper and liquid smoke. (Skip step if already cooked)..
  3. Mix carrots and peas in a separate pot with water and butter. Warm until butter is fully melted. Drain..
  4. Now in a large mixing bowl...mix all ingredients together. Toss. Add a little more EVOO and vinegar to taste..
  5. You can also add some powdered parmesean cheese if you like..
  6. This can be eaten hot or cold..
