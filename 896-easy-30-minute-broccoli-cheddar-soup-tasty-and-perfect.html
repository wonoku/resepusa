Ingredients of EASY 30 minute Broccoli cheddar soup

  1. Prepare 1/3 cup of real butter.
  2. It's 1 of purple onion chopped fine.
  3. Prepare 4 cloves of garlic minced fine.
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup of flour.
  5. You need 2 cups of chicken broth.
  6. Prepare 3/4 teaspoon of salt.
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of pepper.
  8. You need 3 cups of half and half or whole milk.
  9. It's 1 teaspoon of garlic powder.
  10. It's 1 of large broccoli crown cut into small florets.
  11. It's 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese (grate it off the block for better melting).
