Ingredients of 健康黄姜鸡饭 Healthy Biryani Chicken Rice

  1. It's of 鸡肉 Chicken (250g).
  2. Prepare of 印度长米 Basmati rice (1 cup).
  3. You need of 红萝卜 Carrot (1/2).
  4. Prepare of 洋葱 Onion (1).
  5. You need of 蒜米 Minced garlic (1 tablespoon).
  6. You need of 肉桂 Cinnamon stick (1).
  7. You need of 八角 Star anise (2).
  8. Prepare of 黄姜粉，辣椒粉 Turmeric powder, paprika (1 tablespoon each).
  9. It's of 胡椒粉 Pepper powder.
  10. It's of 牛油 Butter (1 small cube).

健康黄姜鸡饭 Healthy Biryani Chicken Rice instructions

  1. 用黄姜粉，辣椒粉，胡椒粉，牛油腌鸡肉5-10分钟 Marinate chicken with turmeric powder, paprika, pepper powder, butter for 5-10mins.
  2. 红萝卜切丝，洋葱切丁 Cut carrot into thin slices, cut onion into small cubes.
  3. 把所有材料放入电饭锅或压力锅 Put all the ingredients into rice cooker or pressure cooker.
