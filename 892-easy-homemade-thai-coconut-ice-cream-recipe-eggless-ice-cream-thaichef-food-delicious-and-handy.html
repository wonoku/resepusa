Ingredients of 🧑🏽‍🍳🧑🏼‍🍳 Easy Homemade Thai Coconut Ice Cream Recipe • Eggless Ice Cream |ThaiChef food

  1. You need 400 g. of Coconut milk.
  2. It's 1 tbsp. of Corn starch.
  3. You need 4 tbsp. of Sugar.
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp. of Salt.

🧑🏽‍🍳🧑🏼‍🍳 Easy Homemade Thai Coconut Ice Cream Recipe • Eggless Ice Cream |ThaiChef food step by step

  1. Mix coconut milk + sugar + salt + corn starch together • continue stir until thicken and let's cool transfer to container cover nicely and place in freezer 4 hours.
  2. After 4 hours take out from freezer and stir mixture for smooth texture •place in freezer 6 hours and take out blend until smooth then place in freezer over night.
  3. Done...! 🥥 eat with thai condiment • sticky rice + toddy palm and on top with evaporated milk + roasted peanut.
