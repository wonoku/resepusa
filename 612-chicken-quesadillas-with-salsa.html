Ingredients of Chicken Quesadillas with salsa

  1. Prepare 250 gr of chicken breasts.
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of ground red onion and garlic.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of ground paprika.
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp of chilli.
  5. It's 1/2 tsp of oregano.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of black pepper.
  7. It's of Salt.
  8. You need 1/2 of red paprika (slice it).
  9. It's 1/2 of Onion (slice it).
  10. You need 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  11. Prepare 1 cup of cheddar cheese.
  12. You need 4 of large tortillas.

Chicken Quesadillas with salsa step by step

  1. Cut the chicken breasts in to dice size.
  2. Season chicken breasts with red onion, garlic, paprika, chilli, oregano, black pepper and salt.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a non stick skillet.
  4. Add the chicken, and cook it until cooked through.
  5. Add Onion and Paprika.
  6. Remove the chicken and place it into a plate.
  7. Preheat a large non stick skillet over medium high heat.
  8. If it's already hot enough, place the tortilla in the non stick skillet for 2 minutes, and then flipp it.
  9. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on one half of the tortilla, followed by chicken, paprika, and onion, and the last add some mozzarella cheese.
  10. Fold it in half.
  11. Let it cooked for 3 minutes more each side.
