Larry's easy 1 pan chicken and potato bake instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 360.
  2. Prepare large baking pan, spray with non stick pam..
  3. Use one packet of Italian dressing seasoning as a dry rub on the breast..
  4. Put the cut seasoned chicken in the greased pan long way down center..
  5. Put drained green beans on one side of the chicken in pan as seen in pic..
  6. Put cut potatoes on other side of chicken in pan as seen in pic..
  7. Add sliced onion over potatoes and chicken..
  8. Sprinkle other Italian seasoning package over entire pan.
  9. Drizzle melted butter over entire pan.
  10. Cover pan with foil..
  11. Bake at 360 for 1:10.
  12. Serve and enjoy!.
