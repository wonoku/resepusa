Ingredients of Easy chicken fajitas

  1. Prepare 4 tbs of cooking oil.
  2. Prepare 1 tsp of garlic and ginger powder or garlic and ginger paste.
  3. You need of Salt to your liking for veggies and chicken.
  4. It's of Black pepper for both chicken and veggies.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of Plain Yogurt or cream.
  6. It's 2 tsp of lemon juice.
  7. You need 1 tsp of dried oregano.
  8. You need 1 tsp of ground cumin.
  9. It's 2 tsp of chili powder.
  10. You need 1/2 tsp of paprika.
  11. It's 1 1/2 tsp of crushed red pepper flakes.
  12. Prepare 1-1/2 pounds of boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into strips.
  13. You need 1 of medium sweet red, green, and yellow peppers, cut into strips.
  14. Prepare 1 of medium onion, cut into strips.
  15. You need of flour tortillas, warmed.
  16. You need of Topping like sour cream, salsa, lemon, etc.

Easy chicken fajitas instructions

  1. In a large bowl, marinate your chicken with oil (half the oil), lemon juice and the seasonings to the chicken. refrigerate your chicken for a few hours.(optional, but its better if you refrigerate the chicken. it adds more flavore to it).
  2. In a nonstick skillet, saute peppers and onions in oil cook until tender add a little salt and pepper then remove..
  3. In the same skillet, add some oil then put in your chicken. cook the chicken over medium-high heat for 4 to 6 minutes or until its no longer pink. Put in the veggie mixture to the pan (taste (season to your liking), wait for a few minutes then remove..
  4. Fill the center of tortillas and fold, you can serve with cheese, salsa, sour cream, taco sauce, and guacamole (optional) ENJOY!! Note: make sure to taste before you serve,add or reduce seasonings as much as you want..
