Ingredients of Spiced Chicken Breast

  1. Prepare 1 of chicken breast skinless.
  2. You need 1 tbsp of coconut oil.
  3. Prepare of DRY SPICE RUB OPTION 1.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of ground cumin.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of ground coriander.
  6. You need 1 tsp of ground turmeric.
  7. It's 1 tsp of paprika powder.
  8. It's 1 dash of salt and pepper.
  9. It's of DRY SPICE RUB OPTION 2.
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp of curry powder.
  11. You need 1 tsp of sweet paprika.
  12. Prepare 1 tsp of chili pepper.
  13. You need 1/4 tsp of salt and pepper.

Spiced Chicken Breast step by step

  1. Butterfly chicken breast then set aside.
  2. I USED DRY SPICE RUB OPTION 2 ,,,,,Mix all spice and press to coat chicken.
  3. With little coconut oil pan grill or bake it till lightly brown and cooked on both side for about 5 minute or longer depending on the thickeness of the chicken then leaving rest.
  4. Serving option slice it up and serve with mustard spaghetti and enjoy.
