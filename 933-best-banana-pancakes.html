Ingredients of Banana Pancakes

  1. You need 1 of mashed banana.
  2. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of flour.
  4. You need 1/8 tsp of baking powder.
  5. It's 1/8 tsp of kosher salt.
  6. You need 1/4 tsp of vanilla extract.
