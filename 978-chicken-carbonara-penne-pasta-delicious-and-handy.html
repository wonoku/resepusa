Ingredients of Chicken carbonara penne pasta

  1. It's 2 of Chicken breasts.
  2. It's 6 slices of bacon.
  3. It's 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  4. You need 6 cloves of garlic.
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.
  7. You need 1/2 teaspoon of paprika.
  8. It's 12 oz of penne pasta.
  9. You need 3 cups of water.
  10. You need 1 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese.
  11. It's 2 of eggs.
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup of sweet corn/green peas.
  13. Prepare of parsley (for garish).

Chicken carbonara penne pasta step by step

  1. Mix salt pepper and paprika and season both sides of chicken breasts. Dice bacon and mince garlic..
  2. Preheat cast iron skillet on high flame, reduce to medium flame then add oil.
  3. Cook both sides of the chicken until done. An internal temperature of 165F is ideal..
  4. Cook the bacon until crispy then add garlic and cook until fragrant..
  5. Remove the bacon, garlic and bacon grease from the pan..
  6. Add the water to the pan and cook pasta on medium flame for 10 minutes. Then reduce to a low flame..
  7. Combine the two eggs and parmesan cheese then immediately add to the pasta. Ensure to constantly stir to evenly coat pasta with the sauce and to prevent it from clumping/scrambling..
  8. While stirring, add chicken, bacon and garlic, and sweet corn or green peas. Garnish with parsley and serve hot..
