Ingredients of Easy orange chicken Stirfry

  1. Prepare 4 of chicken breasts.
  2. You need of Chinese style Stirfry blend.
  3. It's of Orange sauce.
  4. You need 1 of & 1/2 cup brown rice.
  5. It's 2 cups of flour.
  6. It's to taste of Salt & Pepper.
  7. You need 2 Tablespoons of butter.

Easy orange chicken Stirfry step by step

  1. Cook 1 1/2 cup of rice (follow the directions on the box or bag).
  2. Dice chicken.
  3. Mix flour, salt and pepper in a bowl. Dredge chicken in flour mix (lightly cover chicken flour mix).
  4. Add 2 Tablespoons of butter to a pan on medium heat. Cook all of your chicken, then set it to the side..
  5. Cook stir fry blend with 12oz of orange sauce.
  6. Mix chicken and stir fry mix then serve over or next to rice..
