Ingredients of Easy “B.R.C.” Bake (Beans, Rice & Chicken)

  1. You need 2 1/2-3 cups of cooked chicken (cut into small cubes).
  2. It's 2 cups of white rice, cooked.
  3. It's 1 can of pinto beans, drained and rinsed.
  4. You need 1 1/2 cups of frozen corn.
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese, separated in 1/2.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of sour cream.
  7. It's 1/2 cup of salsa (your favorite kind and heat level).
  8. You need 2 tbs of jalapeño, diced.
  9. It's 1 tbs of taco seasoning.
  10. You need of Garnish with chopped cilantro, diced red onion, sliced black olives, avocado slices, lime wedges, and/or salsa.

Easy “B.R.C.” Bake (Beans, Rice & Chicken) instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°..
  2. Thoroughly mix all ingredients (only 1/2 of the cheese) in a mixing bowl..
  3. Pour mixture into a 9x9 baking dish and top with remaining cheese. Place in preheated oven for 25 mins..
  4. Remove from oven and let stand for at least 5 mins. Top with optional garnishes, serve and enjoy!.
