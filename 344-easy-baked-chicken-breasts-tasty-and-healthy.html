Easy baked chicken breasts instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Melt butter.
  3. Dice onion finely.
  4. Place chicken on a rimmed cookie sheet.
  5. Pour melted butter over each breast. Reserve 2 tbsp..
  6. Season each breast with garlic salt.
  7. Evenly place minced onion on each breast..
  8. Sprinkle with paprika..
  9. Bake for 20 minutes..
  10. Remove from oven, pour on reserved butter. Top with chopped parsley. Place back in oven for 10 minutes..
