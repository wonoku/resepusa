Ingredients of Tandoori Chicken rolls

  1. It's 250 g of maida (can substitute with all-purpose flour).
  2. It's 12 gms of Active yeast.
  3. Prepare of Salt.
  4. Prepare 1 of Egg.
  5. It's of Baking soda.
  6. Prepare of For filling:.
  7. Prepare 150 gr of boneless chicken.
  8. Prepare 2 tsp of Red chilly powder.
  9. Prepare 2 tsp of Tandoori masala.
  10. You need of Salt.
  11. It's 1 tsp of Garlic ginger paste.
  12. It's of Mozarella cheese.

Tandoori Chicken rolls instructions

  1. Take maida in a bowl add oil egg soda and active yeast and make dough keep it for one hour..
  2. Take boneless chicken and add above ingredients and cook for 15 mins..
  3. Take a dough and make a roll and add chicken filling in it.add lots of grated cheese..
  4. Bake this at 200 degree celsius for 10mins.
  5. Rolls are ready to eat. U can eat with tomato sauce or mayonisse.
