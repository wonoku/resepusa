Lemon pepper chicken instructions

  2. Then take pan add oil into it then add marinated chicken pieces. Cook it until it changes the colour..
  3. Take a bowl add corn flour egg curd and mix it well. Add few drops of lemon juice to it..
  4. After chicken is cooked add this paste to it. Again cook it for 20 mins until it becomes dry and oil comes out..
  5. Ur dish is ready.
