Buffalo Ranch Chicken Breast instructions

  1. Wash chicken breast and lay them flat in oven pan. Add enough water to almost cover top of the chicken breast..
  2. Add bouillon cubes, onion powder and garlic powder. Cover with tin foil. Put in the oven at 400°F for 30 minutes..
  3. When the chicken breast is tender, drain the water and add the ranch packet and buffalo wing sauce..
  4. Place in oven for 20 minutes then enjoy!.
