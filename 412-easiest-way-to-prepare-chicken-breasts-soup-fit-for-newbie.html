Chicken breasts soup instructions

  1. Cut chicken breasts into medium cubes..
  2. Put breasts into water, add broth cubes..
  3. Cut potatoes into medium cubes, add them to the soup..
  4. Slice cabbage and add it to the soup..
  5. Grate carrot, mince onion, and add them to the soup..
  6. If wanted, cut 2 tomatoes into medium cubes, and add them to the soup. Beware: it will alter soup flavor significantly, so try it only if you really like tomatoes..
  7. Chop leeks, add them to the soup..
  8. Add salt and black pepper to taste. For me, a lot of pepper was good..
  9. Serve and enjoy :).
