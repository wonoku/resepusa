Creamy Kale and Chicken.

You can have Creamy Kale and Chicken using 10 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Creamy Kale and Chicken

  1. Prepare 12 ounces of frozen chopped kale.
  2. Prepare 1-1/2 pound of chicken thighs boneless and skinless.
  3. It's 2 slices of thick bacon.
  4. It's 1 stick of butter.
  5. It's 1/2 cup of buttermilk.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of whole milk.
  7. You need 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour.
  8. You need 1 teaspoon of ground white pepper powder.
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon of salt.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese.

Creamy Kale and Chicken step by step

  1. Melt the butter and fry the bacon till crispy. Chop the bacon up..
  2. Slice the chicken and season with salt and white pepper powder. Then add to the butter and bacon drippings. Allow to sauté for 5 minutes..
  3. Add in the kale and sauté then simmer covered, 12 minutes total. About half and half on time of sauté and simmer..
  4. Add in the 1/2 the flour. Stir in the the other half. Allow to sauté for 7 minutes..
  5. Then add in both milks and let it thicken. Don't overcook the sauce or it will break..
  6. Get the cheese and grate it on top. Cover and allow to melt, before serving. I hope you enjoy!!!.
