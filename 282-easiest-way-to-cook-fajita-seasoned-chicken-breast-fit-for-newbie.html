Fajita seasoned chicken breast step by step

  1. Lightly coat chicken in oil.
  2. Sprinkle on fajita seasoning on both sides and sprinkle on garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  3. Air fry, bake or grill till fully done till the internal temperature is 165° degrees.
  4. Let chicken rest for a few minutes then serve and enjoy.
