Ingredients of Roasted Garlic & Herb Chicken Breasts in A Pressure Cooker

  1. You need of Weber roasted garlic & herb seasoning.
  2. Prepare 3-4 lbs of boneless chicken breasts sliced into 2" strips.
  3. It's 2 cups of brown long grain rice.
  4. You need 1 can of sweet corn.
  5. It's 1 can of black beans drained & rinsed.
  6. It's 1 of sweet green pepper chopped.
  7. It's 1 of sweet onion colored.

Roasted Garlic & Herb Chicken Breasts in A Pressure Cooker instructions

  1. Slice chicken beast into 2" strips.
  2. Liberally sprinkle chicken with Weber Roasted Garlic & Herb seasoning allow to sit for 15 minutes..
  3. Pour 1/2 cup of water into pressure cooker. Add chicken strips seal & cook for 8 minutes..
  4. Remove chicken from pressure cooker & place on plate. Cover with foil..
  5. Pour 3 cups of water & 2 cups of brown long grain rice to pressure cooker. Cook Rice for 12 minutes..
  6. While rice is cooking mix corn, peppers, onion, & black beans into a microwave safe bowl & cook for 5 minutes..
