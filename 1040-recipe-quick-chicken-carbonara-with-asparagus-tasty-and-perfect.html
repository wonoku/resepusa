Ingredients of Quick Chicken Carbonara with Asparagus ðŸ˜€

  1. Prepare 2 of chicken breasts.
  2. You need 310 grams of Carbonara Sauce.
  3. You need 1 bunch of Asparagus cut into thirds.
  4. You need 2 cup of pasta.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of grated parmesan cheese.
  6. You need 1/2 tsp of Black Pepper.
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup of Double cream.

Quick Chicken Carbonara with Asparagus ðŸ˜€ step by step

  1. Boil the pasta then drain and set aside.
  2. Cut chicken breast up long length and in a fry pan heat olive oil, then add chicken, fry until cooked around 15-20 mins..
  3. Cut up asparagus, and fry with chicken for the last 5 mins. Then turn low cover and steam for 5 mins..
  4. When pasta is cooked, drain and pat dry, and add to the chicken and asparagus, also sprinkle in the black pepper..
  5. Pour in carbonara sauce mix well and simmer for a further 5 mins. Add the cream mix and stir for 1 min..
  6. Serve with parmesan cheese sprinkled on top..
