Ingredients of Buffalo chicken quesadilla

  1. Prepare 3 cups of cooked chicken shredded.
  2. It's 1 cup of Franks Red Hot Buffalo Wing Sauce.
  3. It's 1/4 cup of ranch dressing.
  4. It's 4 oz of cream cheese.
  5. Prepare 6 of burrito size flour tortillas.
  6. Prepare 2 cups of Shredded Mozzarella.
