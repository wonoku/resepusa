Roasted Rice and Chicken.

You can cook Roasted Rice and Chicken using 11 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Roasted Rice and Chicken

  1. You need of Rice.
  2. You need 1 cup of white long grain rice.
  3. Prepare 1 stick of butter.
  4. You need 2 cubes of chicken bouillon.
  5. It's of Chicken.
  6. Prepare 2-1/2 pound of chicken thighs bone in and skin on.
  7. It's 2-1/2 tablespoons of ground paprika.
  8. It's 1-1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  10. It's 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder.
  11. You need 2 cup of water.

Roasted Rice and Chicken step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Mix the spices and set aside..
  3. Melt the butter and crush the bouillon cubes. Let the bouillon melt in the butter add the rice and sauté till the rice turns brown about 10-15 minutes..
  4. Add the spice mix to the chicken on both sides..
  5. After it is rubbed in well add the chicken to the rice.
  6. Any spices that fell off the chicken add to the rice. Now add the water to the pan. It should be sizzling.
  7. Turn off the heat and put the pan, skillet into the oven and roast for 45 minutes. Let rest for 11 minutes..
  8. It's ready to serve I hope you enjoy!!!.
