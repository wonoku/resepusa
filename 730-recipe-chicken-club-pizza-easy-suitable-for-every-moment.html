Chicken club pizza EASY instructions

  1. Heat oven to 400 and grease baking sheet. Spread out pizza dough on a 13x9 pan. Bake 8 minutes. Remove from oven..
  2. Mix sauce ingredients and spread on to partially baked crust..
  3. Top with chicken then the cheese then top with bacon, onion and tomato. Return to oven for 6-7 more minutes..
  4. Place under broiler for 3-4 minutes until cheese is speckled. Sprinkle some dried parsley on top for a garnish..
