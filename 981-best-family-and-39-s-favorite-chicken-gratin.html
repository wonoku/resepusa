You can cook Family's Favorite Chicken Gratin using 9 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Family's Favorite Chicken Gratin

  1. It's 300 g of Chicken thigh(cut into pieces).
  2. It's 1 of onion(sliced).
  3. It's 1 pack of mushrooms(sliced).
  4. It's 1 of broccoli(boiled).
  5. Prepare 2 of potatoes(boiled).
  6. It's 60 g of macaroni or penne.
  7. You need 1 of canned or homemade white sauce(300g).
  8. You need 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs.
  9. It's 5 Tbs of grated cheese.

Family's Favorite Chicken Gratin step by step

  1. In a pot of boiling salted water, cook the macaroni, and drain..
  2. Preseason the chicken with salt and pepper..
  3. Heat a pan, put 1 Tbs of oil or butter, sautee the chicken. Once they turn white, add onion and mushrooms, stir them together until onions become translucent. (add white wine if you like).
  4. Add white sauce, boiled macaroni, broccoli and potato into the pan, heat them over low heat until they are nicely mixed..
  5. Put in a casserole or a gratin dish, top it with breadcrumbs and grated cheese..
  6. Grill or bake in the oven until browned. About 10 minutes when grilled, 15 minutes when baked in the 250℃ oven. Done..
