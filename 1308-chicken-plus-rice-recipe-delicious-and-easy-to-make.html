Chicken plus rice recipe step by step

  1. Clean chicken pieces.
  2. In a different pot put in the tamato paste and all the spices add 1/2 of water bring to a boil.
  3. Pour into chicken add a lil salt if the sauce isn't salted to ur taste.
  4. Put chicken in a dish and bake for 30 minutes or till chicken is cooked.
  5. For the rice.
  6. In a pot put oil add in the bay leave and jalapeño and mixed it for a lil add in the onions and garlic than mix till they become soft.
  7. Clean rice until clear water comes out.
  8. Add some tamato paste to the onions mix for a little than add in salt and Arabian mix spice if u have and bring to a boil than lower heat and leave to cook.
  9. Once rice is cooked serve with chicken and sahwik and enjoy.
