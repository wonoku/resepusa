Ingredients of ARROZ CHAUFA De POLLO -- Peruvian Chicken Fried Rice

  1. It's 600 gr of white rice.
  2. You need 300 gr of chicken breast fillets, diced.
  3. It's 90 gr of red paprika/morrón, diced small.
  4. It's 100 gr of onions, sliced lengthwise.
  5. You need 2 tsp of ginger, finely chopped.
  6. Prepare 1 of leek, thinly sliced.
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp of sweet soy sauce.
  8. You need 2 tsp of salty soy sauce.
  9. You need 1 tsp of sesame oil.
  10. Prepare of Vitsin to taste (optional).
  11. You need to taste of Salt and pepper.
  12. You need of Oil for frying.
  13. Prepare of OMELET:.
  14. You need 3 of eggs.
  15. You need 3 tbsp of evaporated milk.
  16. You need of COMPLEMENTARY.
  17. It's 4 of shallots, thinly sliced and fried.
  18. You need 2 tbsp of celery, chopped.
  19. Prepare 120 gr of fried peas.

ARROZ CHAUFA De POLLO -- Peruvian Chicken Fried Rice step by step

  1. Beat the eggs with milk and a little salt (1 egg + 1 tbsp milk), fry it and then roll. Remove and cut into pieces..
  2. Saute onion and ginger until fragrant, put in chicken pieces. Cook until chicken changes color..
  3. Add rice, red paprika and leek. Mix well..
  4. Add soy sauce, vitsin, salt and pepper. Stir again, add sesame oil shortly before turning off the flame..
  5. Place the fried rice in a serving dish, sprinkle with fried shallots and celery..
  6. Serve with scrambled eggs (omelets) and fried peas..
