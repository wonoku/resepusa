Ingredients of Harvest Sausage Shepherd's Pie

  2. It's 2 of celery, diced.
  3. It's 4 cloves of garlic, minced.
  4. You need 2 of carrots, diced.
  5. Prepare 1/2 of granny Smith apple, skinned and diced.
  6. Prepare 1 of large onion, diced.
