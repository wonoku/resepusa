Ingredients of Tandoori Lamb Chops

  1. You need 9 pieces of - Lamb rib chops.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of - Hung curd.
  3. Prepare 4 tsp of - Shan fried chops Masala.
  4. It's 1/2 tsp of - Turmeric powder.
  5. You need 1/2 tsp of - Red chili powder.
  6. You need 1/2 tsp of - Black pepper Powder.
  7. You need 1 tsp of - Garlic paste.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of - Ginger paste.
  9. You need 1/4 cup of - vegetable oil.
  10. It's of Salt as per taste.
  11. You need 1/2 of lemon - Take out juice from it.

Tandoori Lamb Chops instructions

  1. At first clean and wash Lamb chops properly.Then take a bowl pour the lamb chops in to it..
  2. Add Hung curd & all the spices accordingly (Red chili Powder,Turmeric powder,Shan fried chops Masala,Black pepper powder)in to the bowl..
  3. Add Ginger & Garlic paste,Lemon juice & add salt as per your taste..
  4. Add oil and mix well all the ingredients together until coated properly. Cover the Bowl and keep it in refrigerator for over night or at least 2-3 hours..
  5. Take out the marinated Lamb chops from refrigerator.Heat little oil in a Fry pan place all the Lamb chops on the Fry panÂ & saute them on low to medium heat.Keep turning the lamb chops and fry each side evenly until cooked properly..
  6. When all the sides of the lamb chops cooked well turn off the flame & serve it hot with Onion Cucumber salads..
