Veg gratin !.

You can cook Veg gratin ! using 6 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Veg gratin !

  1. You need of Onion.
  2. It's of Courgette.
  3. It's of Carrots.
  4. It's of Eggs.
  5. You need of Fresh cream/ milk.
  6. You need of Spices.

Veg gratin ! instructions

  1. Fry slices of onion in 2 tbsp of oil till golden.
  2. Add grated courgette and stir.
  3. Add grated carrots and stir again.
  4. Add salt black pepper and cover the pan, let it for five minutes on low fire, after that put it in a mold.
  5. Mix eggs with fresh cream or milk, add salt and black pepper, pour it on veg.
  6. Bake it in oven on 200 degrees till golden.
