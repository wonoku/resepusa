Ingredients of Simple, Easy, Delicious Lemon Garlic Roast Chicken

  1. Prepare 4 pounds of bone-in, skin-on chicken pieces, patted dry.
  2. Prepare 2.5-3 teaspoons of kosher salt.
  3. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.
  4. You need 3-4 cloves of garlic, grated or 2 teaspoons garlic powder (not garlic salt).
  5. You need of zest of 1 lemon.
  6. It's of juice of 1 lemon (about 2 to 2.5 Tablespoons).
  7. It's 1 teaspoon of sugar.
  8. Prepare 1 Tablespoon of dried herbs of choice (I like any combo of parsley, thyme, rosemary) or 3 Tbsp finely chopped fresh herbs.
  9. You need 3 Tablespoons of olive oil.
