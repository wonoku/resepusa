Ingredients of Easy Southwestern Chicken Egg Rolls

  1. Prepare 1 pack of chicken breasts (pack of 2) seasoned your way.
  2. It's 1 (16 oz.) of package of frozen chopped spinach, thawed.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of fresh parsley (chopped).
  4. You need 1 can of Green Giant southwestern corn blend (drained).
  5. Prepare 2 cups of shredded cheese (your choice) not Italian.
  6. Prepare 8 (7 inch) of flour tortillas.

Easy Southwestern Chicken Egg Rolls step by step

  1. Preheat pan with butter or vegetable oil for chicken..
  2. Place chicken breasts in pan then sautee them until done..
  3. Cut up chicken. (No particular size but small enough so it can fit into tortilla.).
  4. Add southwestern corn blend, parsley, spinach, cheese to chicken and heat it on medium-low tossing it making sure it's well blended..
  5. Then fill the tortillas with the mixture and wrap them like burritos and cut them into threes (at an angle). Then put them in the oven @ 350Â° for 15 minutes. Enjoy!!.
