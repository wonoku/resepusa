One pot rice cooker chicken biryani instructions

  1. Combine the chicken and marinade ingredients together in a bowl, cover and rest for a few hours, preferably overnight.
  2. Rinse basmati rice 3 times and then soak for 15 minutes and drain water.
  3. Place the marinated chicken in the bottom of the rice cooker bowl ensuring it is evenly spread across the base.
  4. Top the chicken with the fresh mint, coriander, chilli, and fried shallots..
  5. Later on top with drained rice, water, oil, salt, Star anise, clove, bay leaf, cardamom pod..
  6. Set rice cooker to cook or white rice mode depending on your model. Should take 40 minutes..
  7. When the rice cooker is complete, open and remove the dried spices and add the garnish ingredients. Close the lid and let it steam further under the keep warm function for another 10 minutes..
  8. Fluff the top layer of rice and scoop to the very bottom to get a mix of rice and chicken. Serve hot with raita..
