Ingredients of Simple Fried Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo

  1. It's of Chicken and sauce ---------.
  2. It's 1-1/2 pounds of chicken breast boneless and skinless.
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon of seasoned salt.
  4. It's 1/4 teaspoon of ground white pepper.
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  6. It's 1 tablespoon of parsley flakes.
  7. It's 1/3 cup of all purpose flour.
  8. It's 16 ounces of jar of your favorite Alfredo sauce.
  9. It's of pasta------.
  10. You need 1 pound of Fettuccine noodles.
  11. It's 3 quarts of salty water.
  12. You need 2 tablespoon of extra Virgin olive oil.
  13. Prepare 2 tablespoons of extra Virgin olive oil.

Simple Fried Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo instructions

  1. Bring the water to boil add salt, and olive oil. Add the pasta and cook per instructions on box..
  2. Dice the chicken, add garlic, salt, flour, and pepper it...
  3. Pan fry in as needed oil to fry.
  4. When chicken is done move to paper towels..
  5. Add chicken to the pasta.
  6. Add parsley, and Alfredo sauce. Warm then add the pasta.
  7. Let the pasta warm in the sauce. Mix well but don't break the pasta..
  8. Serve I hope you enjoy!.
