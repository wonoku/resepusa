Ingredients of Honey pecan chicken breast

  1. It's 2 of boneless skinless chicken breast halves 6 oz each.
  2. It's 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  3. It's 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder.
  4. It's 1/8 teaspoon of pepper.
  5. You need 1/8-1/4 teaspoons of cayenne pepper.
  6. It's 1 tablespoon of butter.
  7. You need 3 tablespoons of honey.
  8. Prepare 2 tablespoons of finely chopped pecans.

Honey pecan chicken breast instructions

  1. Pound chicken with a meat mallet to 1/2 inch thickness sprinkle with seasoning.
  2. In a large nonstick skillet heat butter over medium heat brown chicken on both sides cook covered until chicken is no longer pink about 1 to 8 minutes turning once. Drizzle with honey and sprinkle with pecans cook covered until chicken is glazed 2 to 3 minutes.
