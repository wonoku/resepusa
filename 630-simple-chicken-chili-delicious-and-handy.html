Simple Chicken Chili instructions

  1. Fully cook the ground chicken in a small stew pot.
  2. Combine the beans, tomatoes, chili sauce, chili powder in the pot and mix well on med heat..
  3. Continue on med heat until the mix just begins to boil stirring occasionally..
  4. Cover and let simmer on low heat for 15 min.
  5. Serve!.
