Instant pot Portuguese Chicken and Rice.

You can cook Instant pot Portuguese Chicken and Rice using 18 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Instant pot Portuguese Chicken and Rice

  1. It's 3 of quarter parts of chicken.
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce.
  3. It's 1 tablespoon of white cooking wine (can use regular white wine).
  4. It's 1/2 teaspoon of sugar.
  5. You need 2 of small potatoes.
  6. Prepare 3 of carrots.
  7. Prepare 1 of small onion.
  8. You need 3 cloves of garlic minced.
  9. You need 1/2 of red pepper.
  10. It's 1/2 of green pepper.
  11. Prepare 2 of bay leaves.
  12. It's 3/4 cups of coconut milk.
  13. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric.
  14. Prepare 1 teaspoon of cumin.
  15. You need to taste of Salt and pepper.
  16. Prepare 1 tablespoon of oil.
  17. Prepare 1 cup of jasmine rice.
  18. Prepare 1 cup of water.

Instant pot Portuguese Chicken and Rice instructions

  1. Take the first 4 ingredients and combine them. And marinate the chicken for 20 minutes..
  2. While the chicken is marinating, quarter the potatoes, slice the onion, cut the carrots into thirds, and slice the peppers into think slices..
  3. Heat the instant pot to sear with oil. Add onion and garlic.
  4. Add chicken... let the chicken caramelize before turning over. Add the turmeric, cumin, salt and pepper and stir until fragrant..
  5. Add the potatoes, carrots, peppers, bay leaves and coconut milk and stir..
  6. Put rice in a steamer basket then add the water and place in instant pot. Make sure all of the rice is covered with water. Close the lid and set to the chicken setting on your instant pot..
