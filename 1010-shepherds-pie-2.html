Shepherds pie 2 step by step

  1. Peel potatoes and boil them till soft enough to mash.
  2. While potatoes are cooking in a deep friend pan add the 3tbs oil chopped onion and chopped garlic shredded carrot and peas and cook until onions are semi soft.
  3. Add in your ground beef and continue to cook until beef is well cooked and not pink anymore.
  4. Drain the greese out of your meat with strainer and then add your corn and mix well.
  5. Add in the onion powder paprikasprinkle little salt and pepper adobo and mix together.
  6. Add in beef cube maggi 1/4 cup water mix good.
  7. Add in chicken seasoning ketchup bbq sause n mix well n shut stove off.
  8. Drain potatoes once there done and put them back in pot add the butter and mash with hand masher.
  9. Add in 1/4 tsp salt garlic puree mayo parmesean n milk n mix well with hand blender.
  10. In a caserole pot add your beef mix to the bottom n even out.
  11. Add potatoes on top n spread n even out.
  12. Sprinkle shredded cheese bacon bits and chive on top.
  13. Bake in a preheated oven @350 for 45 minutes take out rest 15 minutes and cut in.
  14. Enjoy.
