Ingredients of Pasta Carbonara

  1. You need 200 g of pasta of your choice (spaghetti/fettuccine/penne).
  2. You need 50 g of smoked beef/bacon ham (thickly sliced).
  3. Prepare 1 of medium size onion (sliced).
  4. You need 1 cup of sliced mushrooms of your choice (shimeji/champignon).
  5. It's 1/2 cup of fresh milk.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of grated cheddar cheese.
  7. You need 1 tbs of butter.
  8. You need 1 tbs of olive oil.
  9. It's 1 tbs of all purpose flour.
  10. It's to taste of Salt & pepper.
  11. It's 1 tsp of beef stock (store-bought).
  12. You need 1/2-1 cup of pasta water.
  13. You need of Fresh parsley (chopped).

Pasta Carbonara instructions

  1. Boil the pasta as instructed in the package. Usually around 10 minutes. Save the pasta water..
  2. Melt butter in a skillet. Stir-fry the onions until fragrant, then add bacon ham/smoked beef slices. Follow with the mushrooms, salt-pepper and beef stock. Cook them for 2 minutes..
  3. Pour in half of the pasta water and bring to a boil. Lower down the heat to low. Add the milk and cheese. Adjust the sauce thickness to your liking with all purpose flour..
  4. Lastly, toss the pasta in the sauce. Mix them well..
  5. Turn off the heat, then transfer the pasta to a plate. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley and enjoy!.
