  3. It's 1 bottle of teriyaki sauce.
  4. You need 1 of fresh pineapple.

Chicken & rice pineapple bowl instructions

  1. Cut pineapple in half cut out the guts.
  2. Cut chicken breast into half inch Cubs and cook on medium high heat until done.
  3. When chicken is done add teriyaki sauce to chicken and cook on low to thicken up the sauce.
  4. Last cook the rice and add to the pineapple long side the rice.
