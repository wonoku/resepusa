Ingredients of EASY Cream cheese stuffed zucchini bread

  1. It's 1.5 cups of flour.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of brown sugar.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of white sugar.
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda.
  5. You need 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder.
  6. It's 1.5 cups of grated zucchini (skin left on).
  7. You need 2 teaspoons of vanilla.
  8. You need 1 of egg.
  9. It's 2 teaspoons of cinnamon.
  10. You need 1/2 teaspoon of nutmeg.
  11. Prepare of Filling: 8 oz softened cream cheese mixed with 1/4 cup powdered sugar.

EASY Cream cheese stuffed zucchini bread step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350. In one bowl mix fry ingredients and in another mix the wet ingredients then combine. BARELY MIX. It will look too thick and dry to be a batter but ITS NOT. Scoop half of the batter mix into a greased loaf pan..
  2. Mix cream cheese and powdered sugar together and pour in the center distributing to almost each end. Cover with remaining batter mixture. Sprinkle a little granulated sugar on top and bake 45 minutes..
