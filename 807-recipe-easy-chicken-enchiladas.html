Ingredients of Easy Chicken Enchiladas

  1. Prepare of Large can of chicken breast or 2 shredded chicken breast.
  2. You need 8 oz. of sharp cheddar cheese.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of chopped onions.
  4. You need 8 oz. of sour cream.
  5. You need of Large flour tortillas (microwave 30 seconds).
  6. It's of Fill tortillas with mixture of above ingredients.
  7. You need 1 can of cream of chicken soup.
  8. Prepare 1 can of mild enchilada sauce.
  9. It's 8 of x13 dish-stoneware, glass or tin.

Easy Chicken Enchiladas step by step

  1. Mix cream of chicken soup and enchilada sauce (mix in water to thin).
  2. Pour some of above sauce in bottom of dish. Lay filled tortillas on top. Cover with remaining sauce. Be sure to get sauce between each tortilla so all side are covered. Top with cheddar or Mexican cheese. Place tin foil over dish. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes..
