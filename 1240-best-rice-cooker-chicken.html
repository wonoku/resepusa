Ingredients of Rice Cooker Chicken

  1. Prepare 1 of whole chicken.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of Kikkoman Soy Sauce.
  3. Prepare 1 of whole lemon.
  4. You need 1/4 cup of regular beer.
  5. It's 8 of gloves chopped garlic.
  6. It's 5 tablespoons of honey.
  7. It's 1 cup of water.
  8. It's 3 tablespoons of ground pepper.
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup of sliced ginger.
  10. You need 5 pcs of bay leaf.
  11. You need 5 pcs of star anise spice.
  12. Prepare 5 tablespoons of cooking oil.

Rice Cooker Chicken step by step

  1. Wash chicken properly.
  2. Mix all ingredients and marinate the chicken..
  3. Put cooking oil in the rice cooker..
  4. Put the chicken and pour the rest of marinade mixture and cover..
  5. Turn the chicken every 20 mins. Total of 40 mins. Then it’s done!.
