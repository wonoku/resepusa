Pastrami stuffed boneless chicken breasts instructions

  1. Rinse the chicken breasts very well and open a whole in them wth a sharp knife. Be carful not to open them completely..
  2. Cut the pastrami into very small pieces..
  3. Mix the pastrami with all the other ingedients and spices . Make sure that the chopped tomatoes, onions, and pepper are relatively small too..
  4. Stuff theses ingredients inside the chicken breasts..
  5. Add some curry powder on top to have some oriental flavor..
  6. Put it in the oven or on a grill for about 20 -35 minutes..
  7. Served hot with Rice, Pasta, or bread..
