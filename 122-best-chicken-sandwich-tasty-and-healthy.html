Chicken sandwich instructions

  1. Boil and shred chicken..
  2. Add all ingredients (except for the bread) into shredded chicken and mix well..
  3. Take a slice of bread and place a third of the chicken mixture on slice, cover with another slice. Repeat for the rest of the bread..
  4. Place in sandwich maker and make sandwich. Decorate with tomato and onion..
